Objective

Earn Source by exploring, killing enemies, and defeating other players. Spend Source to build defenses, level up stats, and bank your wealth. The player who earns the most Source each day becomes the Daily Champion and gets to add a new rule to the game.

Earning Source

Walking+1 every 5 seconds
Kill Lion+4 (base)
Kill Ghost+4 (base)
Kill Player+20
Kill Scorched Stag+400
Destroy Building+5
Dawn / Dusk Bonus+2 per kill during transitions
Distance Bonus+2 per 700 units from the safe zone edge
Death Consolation+3

The further you are from the safe zone, the more Source you earn per kill. Risk vs. reward.

Day / Night Cycle

The world uses real wall-clock time and cycles through four phases: Day (lions roam), Night (ghosts haunt), and Dawn/Dusk transitions where both enemy types are active. Dawn and dusk are the most dangerous — but kills during transitions earn bonus Source.

Enemies

🦁
Lion (Day & Dawn) Melee charger. Lions ramp up speed the longer they chase you, and prolonged chases attract reinforcements. They pounce from range, then swipe up close. After attacking they briefly crouch — your window to counterattack. Weak to ranged attacks. They will also attack your buildings to reach turrets that shoot at them.
👻
Ghost (Night & Dusk) Ranged kiter. Ghosts keep their distance and fire slow homing projectiles. They strafe to dodge and periodically burst-fire two rapid shots. While wandering, ghosts phase out and become intangible. Parried ghost projectiles home onto nearby enemies. Weak to melee — close the gap fast.
🦌
Scorched Stag (World Boss) Fire-breathing stag with 300 HP. Breathes fire, charges at players, and deals heavy melee damage. Drops 400 Source on kill. HP persists across server restarts and resets weekly. Coordinate with other players to take it down.

Buildings

■
Wall (10 Source) Solid barrier. Blocks all movement and projectiles. The backbone of any base. Walls are the only building that slowly regenerates HP.
▤
Gate (15 Source) Passable door. You and whitelisted players walk through; enemies and other players are blocked. Ctrl+click to manage the whitelist. Lower HP than walls.
⯁
Turret (80 Source) Auto-targeting cannon. Fires explosive projectiles at nearby enemies and non-whitelisted players. Shots explode on impact with AOE damage. Enemies that get shot by turrets will aggro and attack them. Ctrl+click to manage whitelist.
❤
Bed (20 Source) Healing station & respawn point. Heals you and your party members when nearby. You respawn at your bed instead of the safe zone. One bed per player. Colored with your shirt and pants colors.

Key Locations

⚡
Safe Zone The green circle at the center of the map. No combat or building allowed. You move faster inside and slowly heal. Enemies that touch it are destroyed.
⚠
Buffer Zone The yellow ring surrounding the safe zone. Combat is allowed here, but building is not. This ensures players can always fight their way to safety without being walled off.
🏦
The Banker Deposit Source for safekeeping — banked Source is protected when you die. 10% withdrawal fee, minimum withdrawal of 10 Source.
📜
The Scribe Submit rule suggestions (200 Source) or vote on others' suggestions (50 Source). The top suggestion is shown to the Daily Champion.

Parties

👥
Team Up Ctrl+click another player to invite them. Party members appear blue on the minimap and share Source: the earner keeps 40%, the rest is split evenly. Up to 10 players per party.

The Rule System

👑
Daily Champion Each day at dusk, the player with the most total Source earned is crowned champion. They can write their own rule (stats reset as sacrifice) or adopt the community's top suggestion from the Scribe (keep stats). Rules are implemented by AI and last until the weekly reset.

Stats

⬆
Upgrades (100 Source per level, max 25) Spend Source to level up: Move Speed, Melee Knockback, Melee Range, Projectile Speed, Projectile Range, Lunge AOE, Lunge Distance, Shield Duration, Dash Distance.

Browser tip: PlainScape runs best in Chrome or Edge. Firefox may have lower frame rates due to differences in Canvas 2D acceleration.
